Data acquisition

Download terrain data from the USGS National Elevation Dataset

In the National Map Viewer find and download the highest availble resolution elevation data for your study area. 

In a web browser go to the USGS National Map Viewer.
Search for: North Carolina State University, Raleigh, NC
Select download by bounding box.  
Drag a bounding box around your study area.
In the USGS Available Data for Download popup check the Elevation box and click Next.
Check the ned19_n36x00_w078x75_nc_statewide_2003 dataset with 1/9 arc-second resolution and click Next.
Highlight this dataset in your cart and checkout.
Enter your email address and place your order. 
When you receive a download link download and unzip the dataset.
