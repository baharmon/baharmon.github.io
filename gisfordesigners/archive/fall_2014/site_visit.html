Site Visit

Essential supplies

Each team should bring a camera, a smart phone with GPS or a GPS unit, a map, sketchpads, and pens or pencils.

Hiking gear

Please bring water and wear outdoor clothing, insect repellent, and hiking boots. Depending upon the weather please consider bringing rain gear or sun screen, sun glasses, and a hat. And remember that a walking stick is always a fashionable accessory!

Exploring Lake Raleigh Woods

Each team should visit Lake Raleigh Woods. Your assignment is to record sites of interest such as trailheads, scenic spots, viewpoints, fords, and challenging terrain. For each site please note the latitude and longitude, describe the site and its conditions, and take photographs or draw a sketch. You should follow the lakeside trail from the Alumni Center to the outcrop on the west side of the lake. From here you may turn back or continue to follow the trail around the lake.
