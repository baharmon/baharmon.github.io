Element Design Charrette

Essential supplies

A plan of your trail design, your CNC terrain model, 12" or 18" tracing paper, markers, pens, scales, masking tape, knives, cutting mat, cutting straight edge, glue, balsa wood, and either chipboard, cardboard, or museum board.

Project brief

In this charrette each team will design elements such as signage, seating, footbridges, and scenic viewpoints for their trail.
