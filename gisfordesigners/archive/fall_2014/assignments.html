Assignments

Upload one zip file that includes your last name, first initial and the assignment number in the zip file title. Your zip file must contain the following 3 items:

  • A brief text (at least a paragraph long) reflecting on the GIS and design processes used. This text will describe your general strategy and your understanding of the assignment. In addition, you should describe how the skills and concepts that you learned may be applied to solve similar but new problems.

    Titled: last name_first initial_assign#_comprehension.docx(*.pdf)

    For example: harmon_b_assign1_comprehension.pdf

  • At least one diagram (*.jpg / *.png) that clearly presents the procedures and overall process of assignment.

    Titled: last name_first initial_assign#_diagram.jpg

  • Map image(s) (*.jpg / *.png)

    Titled: last name_first initial_assign#_map.jpg

Submission: Homework is to be submitted through the NC State University Moodle application.

Grading: Writing-20 points; Diagram-20 points; Map(s)-60 points

Academic Integrity Statement: With the exception of team assignments all work that you do should be the individual effort.
