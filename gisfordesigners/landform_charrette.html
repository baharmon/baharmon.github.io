Landform Charrette

Project brief

In this charrette you will design a landform using an iterative creative process of ideation, form generation, analysis, and critique.

Case studies

Collect case studies of landforms in landscape architecture and land art.

Rapid ideation

First sketch a dozen concepts

Develop the most promising concept by sculpting a polymeric sand model

3D modeling

Model the landform as a NURBS surface in Rhino.

Render views of the landform from different vantage points at different times of day

Export the landform for analysis in GIS

GIS

Import the landform into GIS as a digital elevation model

Analyze the landform in GIS. Compute landscape parameters such as contours, slope, aspect, and curvature. If relevant compute viewsheds, trails, hydrology, erosion, and solar irradiation.

3D Print

3D print a model of your landform for study

Critique

Study your 3D printed model, your renderings, and your analyses in order to critique and refine your design. Repeat this process of ideation, 3D modeling, geospatial analysis, and critique as neccessary.
