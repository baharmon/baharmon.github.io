Trail Design Charrette

Essential supplies

A map with your cost surface and trail route, your CNC terrain model, an aerial photograph of your site, a contour map of your site, 12" or 18" tracing paper, 24" or 36" tracing paper, markers (Chartpak, Prismacolor, or Copic), pens, masking tape, and scales.

Project brief

In this charrette each team will design their trail in greater detail. Teams should first critique and refine the route of their trail. Then they should diagram the program, planting, and circulation, and views. Then teams should develop an illustrative plan with planting, paving, bridges, viewpoints, any major grading and retaining walls, and the placement of elements like signage and seating.
