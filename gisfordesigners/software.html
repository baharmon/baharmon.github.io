Software

  • GRASS GIS

    GRASS GIS is a free and open source Geographic Information System (GIS) used for geospatial data management and analysis, image processing, spatial modeling, and visualization. GRASS GIS is all purpose GIS although it is focused mainly on scientific GIS applications involving big data. Download binaries for Windows, Mac, or Linux here. Suggested version: GRASS 7.0.0beta3

  • ArcGIS

    ArcGIS is the industry standard GIS. Download ArcGIS 10.2 through the university here.

  • Rhinoceros

    Rhino® is a NURBS 3D modeler. Use Rhino 5 on a College of Design computer, download the Windows trial version of Rhino 5 here, or download the free Rhino 5 Mac OSX beta here.
