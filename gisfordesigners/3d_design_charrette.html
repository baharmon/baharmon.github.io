3D Trail Design Charrette

Essential supplies

Your CNC-routed terrain model, CNC-routed inverse terrain model, cost maps as a geotiffs, and trail routes as shapfiles.

Project brief

In this charrette each team will design their trail in 3D - grading the topography and testing views. Teams will use their CNC-routed terrain model and inverted terrain model to cast a malleable, yet precise model in sand. This sand model will be used with the tangible geospatial modeling system to dynamically route trails, grade trails, calculate cut/fill, analyze trail slopes, and test viewsheds. A model of the canopy may also be used to explore views and visibility in more depth.
