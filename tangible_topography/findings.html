Initial findings

  • With digital 3D modeling it is challenging to perceive interior space due to the lack of visual and kinaesthetic depth perception

  • With analog modeling the lack of feedback limits ones ability to critique the model and refine the form

  • Overlaying given and scanned contours is confusing and worsens modeling

  • The difference between the given and scanned model improves modeling and enables an iterative learning process

  • Analytics like water flow can help model processes, but can distort form

  • Because hybrid 3D modeling can be intuitive and analytical it can enable rapid iteration, conceptual blending, and thus creative learning
